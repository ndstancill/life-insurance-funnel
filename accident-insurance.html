Once the claim is approved, benefits can pay quickly—often within days. Exact timing varies by carrier.

Yes. These policies are designed to complement life and health insurance by paying cash during treatment or recovery.

Many plans use simplified questions with no exam. Eligibility and limits vary by state and carrier.
